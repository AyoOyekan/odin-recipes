Moimoi:Nigerian bean pudding.

INGREDIENTS.

    For two servings.

  • 1 cup peeled beans
  • 1/4 medium onion(halved)
  • 1 chilli(halved) pepper(tatashe in Nigeria)
  • Ginger
  • 1 spring green onion(optional)
  • 2 eggs (boiled)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil or butter
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • salt to taste

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a blender combine beans ,onions ,1 part red pepper,1 part onion, 1/2 cup water and ginger. Puree
