Moimoi:Nigerian bean pudding.

INGREDIENTS.

    For two servings.

  • 1 cup peeled beans
  • 1/4 medium onion(halved)
  • 1 chilli(halved) pepper(tatashe in Nigeria)
  • Ginger
  • 1 spring green onion(optional)
  • 2 eggs (boiled)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil or butter
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • salt to taste

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a blender combine beans ,onions ,1 part red pepper,1 part onion, 1/2 cup water and ginger. Puree to a fine paste.
  3. Chop left over pepper and onion. Slice up the egg and mince the green onions if using. Set aside
  4. Melt butter. Add in the seasoning cubes and salt. Stir to combine.Add in the pureed beans and raw egg.Mix well using a mixing pin or hand mixer
  5. One an oven tray with culinary parchment. Pour in the moimoi batter and spread out evenly. Shake the pan gently to distribute.Top with chopped ingredients.
  6. bake moimoi for 15 mins(until lifted at the edges and set)
