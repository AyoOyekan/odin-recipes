Yam and Eggs.

INGREDIENTS.

  • 4-6 eggs
  • 2 medium sized tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers(ata rodo in Nigeria)
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 red bell pepper(ata shombo)(chopped)
  • 1/3 cups vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp. salt plus 1 tsp for yam
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 1/2 tsp. curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme

STEPS

Phase 1: Cooking the Yam
  1. Slice the yam into about an inch size and peel the skin off
  2. Rinse in clean water till water runs clear and no residue in water.
  3. Put yam in pan,add enough water to cover it,add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft. Test with a fork, if it goes through ,then the yam shoiuld be soft enough
  4. Drain water from yam and move on to making the egg sauce
Phase 2 : The Egg Sauce
  1. Brteak eggs into a shallow boel,whisk egg till egg white and egg yolk are well combined, add salt and whisk again then set aside.
  2. Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat,add vegetable oil and heat for about 2 mins.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes,peppers and onions and cook for about 4-5 mins or till oil floats on the surface,add salt,seasoning,curry amd thyme. Stir to combine the ingredients and to avoid sticking to the pan.
  4. Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute before stirring.
  5. Stir to scramble the eggs.
  6. Serve egg sauce with boiled yam.
