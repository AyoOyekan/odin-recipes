Jollof Rice: How to Make Naija Jollof Rice.

INGREDIENTS.

  • Red bell pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Sunflower oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Mild curry powder
  • Dried thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Chicken stock
  • Chicken stock cubes
  • Golden Basmati Rice
  • Salt

STEPS

  1. Wash the tomatoes and peppers and onions.
  2. wash the rice and drain in a colander.
  3. Blend a mixture of the tomatoes ,red bell pepper and scoth bonnet pepper with half a cup of water until smooth.
  4. Chop the remaining onions ad slice the remaining tomatoes thinly.
  5. Heat the oil in a pan under medium heat,then pour the chopped onions .Cook until the onions start to brown.
  6. Add the blended pepper mix, curry powder,thyme,seasoning cubes and bay leaves into a pan.Cook for 20 mins and stir occasionally
  7. Add the tomoto paste and the sliced tomatoes,reduce the heat and allow to simmer until the moisture reduces significantly and the oil seperates to settle on the surface
  8. Bring back the heat to medium then add the washed rice and the stock and two cups of water with salt to taste.
  9. Cover the pot ,allow the rice to boil then reduce the heat slightly. After 15 mins or a bit more check on the rice to see if all the water ha been absorbed.
  10. Stir through with a wooden spoon and cover for a few mins before serving.
  11. Your delicious jollof rice can be served with chicken,turkey, beef and coleslaw.
